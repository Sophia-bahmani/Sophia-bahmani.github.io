Home

Hi, I’m Sophia. Welcome to my tiny NLP corner on the internet. I’m interested in how we can teach machines to understand (or at least model) human language – from classic n‑gram models to modern large language models.

On this site you can find information about my teaching, education, CV, and publications. I’m especially excited about projects that connect linguistics with neural language models.

Example idea: measuring how often a phrase appears in a corpus as an n‑gram versus how an LLM predicts it. Think of it as comparing P("language" | "natural") in data vs. in a model.
